B.J. Habibie

The 3rd President of Indonesia

Here's a time line of B.J. Habibie's life:

  • 1936 - Born in Parepare, South Sulawesi, Indonesia
  • 1960 - Received an engineer's degree in Germany with the title Diplom-Ingenieur; Remained in West Germany as an aeronautics researcher and production supervisor
  • 1962 - Married with Hasri Ainun Besari
  • 1965 - Delivered dissertation in aerospace engineering and received the grade of "very good," and received the title Doktoringenieur
  • 1976 - Become Indonesia government adviser and chief of a new aerospace company
  • 1978 - State Minister of Research and Technology and Chair of Agency for the Assessment and Application of Technology in the Third Development Cabinet
  • 1983 - State Minister of Research and Technology and Chair of Agency for the Assessment and Application of Technology in the Fourth Development Cabinet
  • 1988 - State Minister of Research and Technology and Chair of Agency for the Assessment and Application of Technology in the Fifth Development Cabinet
  • 1993 - State Minister of Research and Technology and Chair of Agency for the Assessment and Application of Technology in the Sixth Development Cabinet
  • 1998 - Vice president in the Seventh Development Cabinet
  • 1998 - President in the Development Reform Cabinet
  • 1999 - End of presidency
  • 2019 - Died with heart problem namely cardiomyopathy
